Part 1: Explosion
Absentation: brother leaves to protect the village 
Interdiction: main character gets the warning that his brother has turned bad and will hurt him
Violation: the main character is hated on by the brother and declared weak

Part 2: Rising Action
Lack: the brother has hurt the main character's family very badly
Departure: the main character decides to take revenge on the brother and sets out to avenge his family 

Part 3: Climax
Struggle: in the fight, the brother protects the main character from a bigger evil 
Branding: the brother and main character join forces to fight the big evil

Part 4: Falling Action
Liquidation: the main character initially thought that his brother is the bad person but he finds the truth that the brother is trying to protect him and they join hands to defeat a greater evil
Return: the brother and main character return together 

Part 5: Resolution
Unrecognized Arrival: the village rejects the brother because he had acted bad initially 
Recognition: the brother and main character explain the whole problem and how the brother has to act bad to protect them and finally get recognised
